\entitle{The Impact of Digital Economy Development on Provincial Employment Structure in China: An Empirical Study Based on Panel Threshold Model}
\enauthor{Author}
\eninstitute{School of Economics}
\enabstract{This paper examines the impact of digital economy development on provincial employment structure using a balanced panel of 31 Chinese provinces from 2011 to 2023. Employing a two-way fixed effects model and the Hansen (1999) panel threshold model, we find that: (1) digital economy development significantly increases the share of tertiary sector employment ($\beta=0.277$, $p<0.01$), and this result is robust across six progressive specifications and five robustness checks; (2) the employment structure effect exhibits regional heterogeneity, with the central region showing the largest marginal effect ($\beta=1.781$, $p<0.05$); (3) the broad digital economy composite index has a stronger effect than the narrow digital finance index alone. These findings suggest that digital transformation, rather than fintech development alone, is the primary driver of employment structural upgrading in China.}
\enkeywords{Digital Economy; Employment Structure; Panel Threshold Model; Regional Heterogeneity}
\jel{JEL Classification: J21; O33; R23}

\begin{abstract}
本文利用 2011--2023 年中国 31 个省份面板数据，以自建数字经济综合指数为核心解释变量，采用双向固定效应模型和 Hansen（1999）面板门槛模型，实证检验数字经济发展对省际就业结构的影响。研究发现：（1）数字经济发展显著提升了第三产业就业占比，在控制省份和年份双向固定效应后，数字经济指数每提高 1 个单位，三产就业占比提高约 0.28 个单位，且该结果在 6 种渐进控制规格和 5 项稳健性检验下保持稳健；（2）数字经济的就业结构效应存在区域异质性，中部地区的边际效应最大（$\beta=1.781$），东部次之（$\beta=0.304$），西部不显著；（3）广义数字经济综合指数对就业结构的影响强于狭义数字金融指数，后者在双向固定效应下不显著。本文的边际贡献在于将数字经济就业效应研究的观测窗口延长至 2023 年，并从门槛效应视角提供了城镇化调节作用的初步证据。

\keywords{数字经济；就业结构；面板门槛模型；区域异质性；双向固定效应}
\end{abstract}
